01 · Studio

Architecture Built on Listening & Craft

D2 — Dedicated 2 Design is a luxury architectural studio founded by Ar. Revathi Velu. We specialize in sustainable, bespoke spaces that reflect the unique stories of the people who inhabit them.

Practicing across Bengaluru, Chennai, Madurai, Coimbatore and Hyderabad, we approach every project — whether residential, commercial or landscape — with the same commitment to thoughtful, enduring design.

"Every space holds a story. We help you tell it beautifully."
